"...if it is not vernacular, what else is there? Vernacular is your mother's tongue—that is your nature."

Baba Demas, December 2023

The Story

We remember our three-hour session in Ibadan. Encountering in person someone we had only read about in Visual Arts classes.

To Baba, vernacular is the natural language of design.

His Roots, His Future

Baba sought to root modern art in the soil of Nigerian life—its materials, rhythms, and cosmology.

He saw design not as a borrowed language, but as a living dialect of place.

The Art Piece

A man shaped by soil. And shaping with soil.

Simply a silent revolution.
